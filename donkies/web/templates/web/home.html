How It Works

Automatically Pay Off Student Debt Everytime You Use Your Debit Card. We Will Round Up Every Transaction To The Nearest Dollar And Use The Balance To Pay It Off.

Benefits Of Using Donkies

Automatic Debt Payback

Sign up once and we will take care of the rest, all you have to do is monitor and let us know when you want your funds transfered to your student loan account.

Increase Networth

As you use your change to pay back student loans, watch your networth increase, making it easy to participate in the things you enjoy the most.

Save Years

Think about it, you can save a good amount of time and not have to pay back student loans. Instead of paying 10 years, why not 7?
